# Analysis Requests

1. **Weather Conditions (Interlagos 2023)**
   Retrieve data for the weather conditions in Races type Races for the Grand Prix Interlagos 2023.  

2. **Race Winners (Oct–Dec 2023)**  
   Analyze all the winners of the Grand Prix races held between October and December 2023.  

3. **Full Throttle Comparison**  
   Compare the time spent at full throttle by Driver X vs their teammate, considering only green flag conditions.  

4. **Braking Time in Fastest Qualifying Lap**  
   Determine how much time Driver X spends braking during their fastest qualifying lap.  

5. **Max Speed with DRS Open**  
   Compare the maximum speed of Driver X with DRS open vs their teammate with DRS open.  

6. **Max Speed with DRS Closed**  
   Compare the maximum speed of Driver X with DRS closed vs their teammate with DRS closed.  

7. **Gap Before First Pit Stop**  
   Calculate the time gap between Driver X and the race leader before Driver X’s first pit stop.  

8. **Gap After First Pit Stop**  
   Calculate the time gap between Driver X and the race leader after their first pit stop.  

9. **Pit Stop Duration vs Teammate**  
   Compare the pit stop duration of Driver X with that of their teammate.  

10. **Pit Stop Duration vs Leader**  
    Compare the pit stop duration of Driver X with that of the race leader.  

11. **Tire Compound After First Pit Stop**  
    Identify the compound used by Driver X after their first pit stop.  

12. **Average Lap Time by Compound**  
    Compare Driver X’s average lap times when using compound X vs compound Y.  

13. **Wind Conditions in Practice & Qualifying**  
    Record wind speed and direction during practice sessions and qualifying.  

14. **Average RPM Shifts**  
    Compare the average gear shift RPM between Driver X and Driver Y.  

15. **Coasting Time Comparison**  
    Compare the time spent coasting (no throttle, no brake) between Driver X and Driver Y.  

16. **Non-DRS Time in Eligible Zones**  
    Measure the time spent without DRS in eligible zones by Driver X vs their teammate during the race.
